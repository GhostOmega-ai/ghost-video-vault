# 👻 Ghost Video Vault

## v0.3 — Immersive Gesture Player

- Minimal floating white controls with Ghost glow
- Ghost-themed Back and Video Info controls
- Rotate icon in the top-right corner of the video
- Thin progress bar with mute to the right and fullscreen below
- Single tap while hidden shows controls
- Single tap on empty video space while visible hides controls
- Central Play/Pause icon
- Double-tap left third to rewind 10 seconds
- Double-tap right third to forward 10 seconds
- Swipe left/right to change videos without exiting
- New video auto-plays and updates its title/count
- Adaptive 1–4 video album-cover layouts retained

Existing videos, albums, favourites, Private pins and covers remain compatible.
